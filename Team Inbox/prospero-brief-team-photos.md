# Brief for Prospero — Team Photos: Visual Descriptions

**From:** Adama
**Project:** Team Photos
**Your deliverable:** Visual descriptions for all 7 team members

---

## What I Need

For each of the 7 current team members, write a visual description that will be handed to Cicero to craft Midjourney prompts. These descriptions will ultimately produce portrait images used as thumbnails in the team roster UI.

## The Style Context

The images will look like **candid black & white surveillance photographs from a 1960s spy movie dossier**. Think grainy film, high contrast, caught-in-the-moment. Each photo should feel like it belongs in the same dossier but have its own distinct character — different angles, expressions, settings, body language.

## How to Approach the Descriptions

You know these team members better than anyone. Draw on that understanding.

- Capture the **essence of each role and personality** — not a literal depiction of the fictional namesake
- A slight nod to the namesake is welcome, but the result should read as a **real person** doing a real (if shadowy) job — not a TV character or mythological figure
- Think: what does this person *look like* when they're in their element? What does their bearing say about who they are?
- Ground the description in the dossier world: clothing, posture, environment, expression — all filtered through the 1960s surveillance aesthetic
- Include enough detail for Cicero to work with: approximate age, build, face/expression, what they're doing or where they are, any telling details

## Example of the Right Tone

*Not this:* "Prospero is a wizard with a long white beard in robes."
*More like this:* "A sharp-eyed man in his 50s, lean and precise, photographed through a café window. He's reading something, but his attention is elsewhere — like he's already three steps ahead. Rumpled suit, no tie. The kind of man who notices everything and reveals nothing."

One section per team member. No need for polish — these are working notes for Cicero.

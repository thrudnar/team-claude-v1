# Team Presentation — Project Brief

## Objective

Produce a set of presentation-ready materials that explain how the AI team works: how Adama orchestrates, how agents are hired and onboarded, what each team member does, and how they connect to each other.

## Deliverables

### 1. Adama Orchestration One-Pager
One-page description of the orchestration role Adama has. How Adama receives work, plans projects, preps tasks, identifies the right agent, briefs agents for work, and orchestrates deliverables from multiple agents.

### 2. Onboarding Process One-Pager
One-page description of the onboarding process, starting with Adama identifying that the team roster doesn't yet include the necessary skill, all the way through completion.

### 3. Onboarding Process Flowchart
A one-page visual flowchart of the onboarding process with swim lanes for the major players. (FigJam)

### 4. Team Roles One-Pager
One-page summary of all team roles — who they are, what they do, their domain.

### 5. Team Role Graph Diagram
Diagram with each agent as a node and connecting lines (edges) between them. Edges are derived from the documented role descriptions. Each line is labeled nearest the requester with the types of requests they route to the agent on the other end. (FigJam)

### 6. Plan Review Checklist Evolution (DONE)
Document showing how the orchestration process evolved through three iterations of the same planning exercise, with coaching notes and flowcharts for each attempt.
